Try It Yourself!

Let's navigate to a folder and see hidden files on your computer.

1 Open Terminal
Press Cmd + Space to open Spotlight, then type Terminal and press Enter.
2 Check where you are
Type pwd and press Enter. This shows your current folder location.
3 Navigate to your home folder
Type cd ~ and press Enter. The ~ is a shortcut for your home folder.
4 See what's in your home folder
Type ls and press Enter. You'll see your Documents, Desktop, Downloads folders, etc.
5 Now reveal the hidden files!
Type ls -a and press Enter. You should see files starting with a dot like .bashrc or .zshrc - those are hidden files!
